About Us

Our Philosophy

The Talati Group started as a food marketing firm in 1994 and navigated our way into the Product Manufacturing Industry by 2002.
Bridging the gap between traditional eating and modern-day lifestyle, we have created the simplest yet most affordable and necessary products required in every Indian household and across the World.
We connect the World with our spices, desserts, flours, and our famous instant mixes.

Infrastructure and Design

Talati's modern infrastructure and robust design set us apart. Our large-scale production house caters to the Food and Hospitality Industry. We also have a strong distribution network in the domestic and local supplies domain.

Our in-house design unit assures that every product is delivered on time, ensuring a smooth demand and supply flow.

Authentic Ingredients

Talati’s warehouse design facilitates separate premises for each product and ensures that our final product retains its natural flavors and color. We rely on the best and most resourceful vendors that provide high-quality raw materials.

The ingredients utilized in Talati products are stored with utmost care ensuring hygiene protocols at all times. Every stage of production has quality checks at regular intervals ensuring that we deliver superior quality products.
